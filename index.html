TRIOE online firmware uploader

pre-requisite: A browser, type C data cable, and a TRIOE Board.This will not work on other boards. Learn more from https://trioe.dev

note: This will not work on other boards. Also, the current codes are not editable as of the moment. Stay tuned for updates.
